How to Use HeadSynth

Welcome to HeadSynth - a musical instrument you can play with head movements!

Getting Started

  1. Click "Enable Camera" to turn on your webcam.
  2. Click "Calibrate" and follow the instructions to set up head tracking.
  3. Click "Start" to begin making music.
  4. Use head movements to control sounds:
Moving your head left/right selects different notes
Moving your head up/down controls volume and intensity
Tilting your head controls effects

Troubleshooting

  • If the camera doesn't activate, check your browser permissions
  • Try a different browser if you continue having issues
  • Ensure you have good lighting for better face tracking

Accessibility Options

Use the sensitivity slider to adjust how much head movement is needed to control the instrument.

HeadSynth works with screen readers and other assistive technology.
